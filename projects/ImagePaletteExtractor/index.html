How to Use Color DNA Extractor

Step 1: Upload Image

Drag and drop any image file or click to browse. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF.

Step 2: Adjust Settings

Choose palette size (3-12 colors), select algorithm, and adjust sensitivity for different results.

Step 3: Extract Colors

Click "Extract Color DNA" to analyze the image and generate a color palette.

Step 4: Use Colors

Click any color to copy its code. Check accessibility contrast and export in various formats.
