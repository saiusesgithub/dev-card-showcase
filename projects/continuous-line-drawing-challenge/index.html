How It Works

  • Click "Start Drawing Challenge" to begin the timer and scoring
  • Draw any object on the canvas without lifting your mouse button
  • If you release the mouse button, the game ends immediately
  • Your score increases the longer you draw and with more complex shapes
  • After game over, watch a replay of your drawing path
  • Try to create recognizable objects with a single continuous line!
