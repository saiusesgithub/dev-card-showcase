How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or buttons to move the player (blue square) through the maze
  • Collect all green items to advance to the next level
  • Avoid red enemies - they'll chase you using pathfinding algorithms
  • Collect yellow power-ups to temporarily freeze enemies
  • Each level increases difficulty with more enemies and complex mazes
  • You have 3 lives - lose one when caught by an enemy

Tip: You can also use WASD keys or arrow keys on your keyboard
